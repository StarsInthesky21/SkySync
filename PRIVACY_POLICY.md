# SkySync Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 9, 2026

## Overview

SkySync ("the App") is a social stargazing application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Username**: A randomly generated display name that you may customize. No real name is required.
- **Chat Messages**: Messages you send in Sky Rooms and Global Chat are stored to enable the social experience.
- **Custom Constellations**: Star patterns you create are stored within your Sky Room.
- **Space Notes**: Notes you attach to celestial objects in shared rooms.

### Data We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Statistics**: Badge progress, challenge completions, XP earned, and discovery counts are stored locally on your device.
- **Authentication**: If Firebase is enabled, we use anonymous authentication. No personal information is required to use the app.

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your profile, badges, challenge progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
- **Cloud Storage** (optional): If Firebase is enabled, room data, chat messages, and user profiles may be synced to Google Cloud Firestore servers.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Contact lists or phone numbers
- Photos, camera, or microphone recordings in the current build
- Financial or payment information
- Browsing history

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase** (Google): Used optionally for authentication and real-time data sync. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Expo**: Used as the development framework. No user data is sent to Expo servers in production builds.

## Children's Privacy

SkySync does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and random usernames by default.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete your data** at any time using Settings > Clear All Data
- **Use the app offline** without any cloud data collection
- **Choose your username** without providing real personal information

## Data Retention

- Local data persists until you clear it or uninstall the app
- Cloud data (if Firebase enabled) is retained while your account exists
- Chat messages in rooms are stored for the lifetime of the room

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at: privacy@skysync.app
