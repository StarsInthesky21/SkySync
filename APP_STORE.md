## Description

Discover the night sky like never before. SkySync is the social stargazing app that lets you explore stars, planets, satellites, and meteor radiants in real time -- alone or with friends.

**Interactive Sky Map**
Drag to rotate and pinch to zoom through a stunning night sky. Tap any celestial object to discover its mythology, scientific facts, and distance from Earth. See animated 3D previews of planets and stars.

**Time Travel**
View the sky from any date between 1800 and 2100. Watch how constellations shift over centuries. Switch viewpoints between Earth, Mars, and the Moon.

**Stargaze Together**
Create private Sky Rooms and invite friends with a room code. Share the same sky view, highlight objects for each other, leave space notes, and chat in real time.

**Draw Constellations**
Connect stars to create your own constellation patterns. Save them to your room and share with friends.

**Earn Badges & XP**
Complete daily challenges to earn XP. Discover planets, trace constellations, and track satellites to unlock badges. Build your streak and level up.

**Voice Lounge Preview**
Coordinate a shared observing session from inside your room while native in-app voice stays in beta.

**Rich Mythology**
Every celestial object comes with mythology stories from cultures around the world, plus animated story sequences for major constellations.

**Works Offline**
Your profile, badges, and progress are saved locally. Stargaze anywhere, anytime.
