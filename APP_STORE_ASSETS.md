### Description

Explore the night sky like never before. SkySync is a social stargazing app that transforms your phone into a window to the cosmos.

**Point & Discover**
Hold up your phone and let the compass guide you to stars, planets, and satellites visible above you right now. Tap any object to discover its mythology, scientific facts, and distance from Earth.

**Real Star Catalog**
Over 500 real stars from the HYG astronomical database, plus all major planets, the ISS, and meteor shower radiants. See accurate positions calculated from orbital mechanics.

**Stargaze Together**
Create Sky Rooms to share your view with friends in real-time. Chat, highlight objects, and draw custom constellations together. Browse public rooms to join other stargazers worldwide.

**Travel Through Time**
View the sky from any date between 1800 and 2100. See how ancient civilizations saw the stars, or preview upcoming celestial events.

**Multiple Viewpoints**
See the sky from Earth, Mars, or the Moon. Experience how the stars look different from other worlds.

**Learn & Earn**
Complete daily challenges to earn XP and unlock badges. Track your streaks, discover all planets, and trace constellations to level up your stargazer profile.

**Live Astronomy Data**
Real-time planetary positions, moon phases, sunrise/sunset times, ISS tracking, and upcoming celestial events like eclipses, meteor showers, and conjunctions.

**Works Offline**
All core features work without internet. Your progress syncs when you reconnect.
